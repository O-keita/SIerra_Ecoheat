Who Are We

Who Are We

Welcome to Sierra Ecoheat, where we’re transforming waste into wonder! At the heart of our mission is a commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship. We harness the power of nature by converting coconut shell waste and sawdust into eco-friendly cooking briquettes, offering a cleaner, greener alternative to traditional fuels.

To provide affordable, accessible, and clean fuel that’s environmentally friendly for households and businesses to reduce the high rate of deforestation in Sierra Leone.

In a world increasingly aware of its environmental footprint, we envision a future where waste is no longer discarded but rather repurposed to fuel progress. Our innovative approach not only tackles the challenge of waste management but also provides a sustainable solution for cooking needs. We believe that every bit of waste has the potential to be a building block for a better tomorrow.
